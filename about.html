Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Hoovu, an exquisite artisanal kitchen and bar that draws inspiration from the rich tapestry of botanical wonders found in the renowned Lal Bagh Park of Bengaluru. In the heart of Bengaluru's cultural ethos, where the vibrant hues of Gulmohar blossoms and the lively flower markets shape the city's identity, Hoovu emerges as a two-tiered culinary haven.

The first level beckons patrons into an opulent realm of fine dining, boasting premium seating amidst an ambience adorned with floral embellishments, providing an exquisite touch of luxury. Immerse yourself in a sensory experience heightened by live art performances in an exclusive corner. Ascending to the second level unveils a casual and laidback atmosphere, offering panoramic views of North Bengaluru—an ideal setting for leisurely gatherings with friends and family

At Hoovu, our skilled mixologists meticulously craft cocktails, utilizing locally sourced ingredients to elevate your drinking experience. Immerse yourself in the essence of Bengaluru as you sip on meticulously curated concoctions, each imbued with the spirit of the locale.

Dedicated to celebrating the essence of Bengaluru, our culinary offerings reflect a commitment to the local ethos. The menu is a harmonious blend of Bengaluru's distinctive flavors, featuring a special selection dedicated to the city's culinary heritage alongside a global cuisine menu. From locally handcrafted honey cakes to indulgent slices of Neapolitan pizza, Hoovu seamlessly marries sophistication with the comforting familiarity of local delights.

Embark on a culinary journey where every detail reflects the vibrancy of Bengaluru, encapsulating the spirit of the city in an ambiance that is both refined and inviting. Hoovu invites you to savor the fusion of opulence and local charm—a gastronomic experience that transcends boundaries and encapsulates the soul of Bengaluru
